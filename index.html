About Me

Greetings! I am Pramit Dutta, a Master of Applied Science (MASc) candidate in Computer Engineering with a focus on Artificial Intelligence at the University of Guelph. I have a bachelor degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology. I am currently working as a Graduate Research Assistant in the AI enabled Medical Imaging Lab under the supervision of Dr. Eranga Ukwatta.

My research primarily focuses on multimodal approaches, with a particular emphasis on Vision-Language Models and their potential in domains like medical imaging. I am interested in developing data-efficient and scalable solutions by using VLMs to align and interpret visual and textual information. My goal is to advance AI-driven healthcare by integrating text and image within multimodal frameworks to enhance model understanding and generalization.
